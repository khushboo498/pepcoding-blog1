What is FAANG And how to get into FAANG?

FAANG is what dreams are made of. Truly. You must have heard that getting hired at FAANG is every coder’s dream. So, what is FAANG and why is it so hankered after by coders? More importantly, How to get into FAANG? In this article you would get to know.

In today’s world, the top few corporations indirectly run the world. FAANG is the acronym for top 5 companies that usually excel in good returns for stocks more than any other company. It is the ultimate cream of corporate giants. This makes them highly sought-after companies for people planning to work in the technical field.

The term originated in 2013 by CNBC Mad Money talk show host Jim Cramer as an abbreviation for Facebook, Apple, Amazon, Netflix and Google. All these companies are trusted to stay relevant for decades to come because of their proven ability to innovate and use technology.

So, an employee would want to work there not only for the world class work environment and hefty salaries but also for job security. Even in the post-pandemic world, these companies continue to hire an aggregate of 923400 employees per month. With Amazon hiring about 35000 employees every year, and Google with its current 135,000 full-time staff.

Engineer at FAANG companies

Engineers at FAANG companies get to collectively work on a single product or a project. Since Facebook, Amazon, Apple, Netflix and Google are all product based companies. They all undertake a rigorous process to select the most exceptional minds with flawless coding skills. And they later enjoy the perks of free meals,

Interview rounds of FAANG

FAANG interviews happen in about 5-10 rounds

Pre Screening interview

For this round you need to create an impressive and attention grabbing resume since your resume gets screened in this round. If your resume gets shortlisted, you would get to the interview where you get asked some basic coding questions and a few HR questions. This round would be relatively easy and if you crack it, you get to the technical test round.

Technical test

In this round they test your grasp on concepts of data structures and algorithms. But there might also be a few difficult problems. The test is conducted on platforms like Hackerrank or the likes.

Clearing the technical test will lead you to the personal interviews.

Personal interview

There will be 2 phases of your personal interviews.

  • Technical interview

    In this round your coding skills would really be tested. The examiner will give you a problem and you have to come up with several solutions. We suggest you make yourself familiar with the most asked questions. Questions asked are usually from DBMS, OS and Computer Networks.

    Along with your coding knowledge, they want to test your problem solving and decision making skills. So make sure to make them aware of your thought process by thinking out loud.

  • HR interview

    In the HR round your soft skills are tested. you may get asked why you are a perfect fit for the role and how compatible you are with the company etc.

Criteria to get into FAANG

To apply at FAANG you either get an opportunity through placement programs in your campus where these companies arrive. In this case you should maintain a CGPA of 7 or 8 as per the criteria of these companies. Maintaining a strong CV profile with plenty of work experience would also help.

If these companies don’t come to your college or university, you can apply for open positions. Connect with the employees of these companies and get referrals from them. Referrals are a great way to get into these companies since these companies prioritise hiring through referrals over consulting firms. Send a personalised message to them via linkedin and mention the job ID and a few things about yourself so you can stand out among the plethora of referral requests they might be getting.

You can also apply on Linkedin where you can stay updated about the job postings. Receiving an interview this way is comparatively unlikely but still worth trying.

The key is to maintain a good CV and profile to show for. Another popular way of applying is through competitions on hackerrank. FAANG companies organise plenty of competitions like Facebook Hacker Cup, Google APAC and more.

How to get into FAANG?

As mentioned before, FAANG are the desired corporations for a lot of technical workers and for good reason. But this has led these companies to have a ridiculous amount of competition for jobs. Yet, it is tough but not unattainable. If you are determined enough and have the right plan, you can ace your interview leaving behind the other less prepared and misguided candidates.

Important areas to prepare are

  • Data structures and algorithms
  • Concepts of operating systems
